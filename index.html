About Us

Universal DEVV Studio is a creative Roblox game studio founded by Arnav and Allesandro, two passionate developers working together to bring players unforgettable experiences. We build unique, fun, and engaging games that make Roblox even better for everyone. Our studio focuses on originality — crafting adventures that stand out from the crowd. One of our creations, Find Him, received love from players and inspired us to keep pushing boundaries. We’re constantly working on new, innovative projects to deliver more excitement and creativity to the Roblox community.
